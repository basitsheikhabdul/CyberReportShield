Aadhaar-based Authentication

Aadhaar-based authentication ensures that every FIR submitted through Cyber Report Shield is verifiably legitimate. By leveraging OTP verification via Aadhaar number, we reduce the risk of impersonation and malicious filing. This method allows users to quickly prove their identity without needing passwords or lengthy forms.

Our OTP system sends a one-time password to the mobile number linked with your Aadhaar ID. Once verified, users gain access to file a FIR that is securely associated with their government ID.
