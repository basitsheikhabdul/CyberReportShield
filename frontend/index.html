How It Works

Filing a First Information Report has never been easier. Follow these simple steps:

1. Aadhaar Verification

Enter your Aadhaar number and verify using OTP to ensure secure access.

2. Submit FIR Details

Write your complaint, add description and optionally upload photo or video evidence.

3. Digital Signature

Review your FIR and submit it securely with digital confirmation.

4. Track FIR Status

Use your reference ID to track the status of your FIR anytime.
