Anonymous Reporting

Cyber Report Shield allows users to file complaints without immediately disclosing their identity. This empowers victims or witnesses to report cybercrime fearlessly, especially in sensitive cases.

Users can later choose to verify their identity through Aadhaar if they wish to convert the complaint into a formal FIR. This feature is useful for whistleblowers, victims of harassment, or anyone hesitant to come forward immediately.

Anonymous reporting fosters a culture of accountability while offering the safety of anonymity and trust.
