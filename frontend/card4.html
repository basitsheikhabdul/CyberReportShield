Digital Evidence Upload

With Cyber Report Shield, users can upload supporting evidence such as photos, videos, or documents during the FIR filing process. This strengthens the credibility of reports and helps authorities act promptly.

The uploaded files are securely stored via Cloudinary and linked directly to the FIR reference ID. Metadata like upload time and file type are preserved to ensure authenticity.

This feature is crucial for cybercrime, online harassment, and any digital incidents where evidence can be captured visually or through media.
